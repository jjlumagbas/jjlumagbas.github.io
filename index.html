I’m transitioning back to full-time industry work after an extended period teaching CS at university. (Before that I was a full-stack web dev for 4 years.)

So I’m a bit of a generalist at the moment, able to take on a range of tasks from UX research and design to front-end development and even some technical team management. (Sure, I can do back-end work as well, just don’t enjoy it as much if I’m honest.)

Through my teaching experience, I’ve become an expert communicator, in writing as well as visually. (I’m no graphic designer, but I am sensitive to visual hierarchy on a well laid-out page.) I love the challenge of taking complex concepts (or data or tasks) and presenting them in a way that’s engaging and easy to grasp.
